What is Stupis screens

✻

Almost person tries to find a perfect and nice looking walllpaper for their device, that results in an endless ammount of searching over the internet and kills a lots of times Most of the time you wont get as unique walllpaper for you. Stupis Screens tries to solve this. It creates qualitiy 2D and 3D graphics using powerfull tools maths for you in a single click. Wallpapers are genaretd randomly so there will not be anyone who will get a same wallpaper as you. Stupis Screens lets you to create 2D wallpapers from various categories called material, exotic, patterns and Flat. In material you will be able to create material desing wallpapers. Exotic offers you a cast ammunt of minimal looking images inlcuding nature, tech and many more. Patterns will create gorgeous looking patterns for you using circles, rects, lines and many more. In Flat you will be able to create flat images with gradients and various images effects. You will also be able to create 3D wallpapers consists of many awsome 3D objects.
