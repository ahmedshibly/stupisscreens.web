What is Stupis Screens

Almost every person tries to find a perfect and nice looking wallpaper for their device, that results an endless amount of searching over the internet and kills a lot of times. Most of the time you won't get as unique wallpaper as you want. Stupis Screens tries to solve this. It generates unlimited high quality wallpapers for you using powerful tools and maths.

Each wallpaper is generated targeting your device resolution, so there is no chance to get a blurry one. Wallpapers are sorted as categories called material, exotic, habijabi and clean. Material offers awesome looking material design wallpapers. Exotic offers you minimal looking wallpaper including nature, tech and many more. Habijabi will create gorgeous looking patterns for you using circles, rects, lines and many more. Clean offers you simple looking wallpapers with various types of gradients.
